A cinematic, physically based particle system with minimal CPU cost. Particle meshes are generated once — all simulation runs entirely on the GPU in a single draw call, making it compatible with nearly every graphics API including OpenGL ES 2.0 and DirectX 9.

  • Physically based motion blur from shutter interval — no post-process required
  • Physically based depth of field: bokeh radius derived from real camera parameters or volumes
  • Energy conservation: fixed per-particle energy over the shutter interval
  • Temporal controls: between-frame blending, closed shutter interval, turbulence
  • Lit and emissive particle shaders
  • Deterministic playback and time reversal
  • Accurate trajectory integrals for gravity, drag, and wind
  • Static lights mode: bokeh and camera motion blur for non-moving light sources
  • Targets OpenGL ES 2.0, DirectX 9 and all modern APIs
